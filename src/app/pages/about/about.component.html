About Us

Welcome to dorea – your one-stop platform for event-based food ordering and catering services. From weddings and birthdays to corporate meetings and intimate gatherings, we deliver delicious meals prepared with love and care.

How It Works:
  • Browse our menu by category: Catering, Sadya, Events, etc.
  • Select dishes from subcategories like Starters, Soups, Main Course, etc.
  • Click “Add to Cart” for each item you wish to order.
  • Once your selection is complete, go to the Cart and review your order.
  • Fill in your event info at Checkout.
  • Submit your request – we'll connect with you on WhatsApp for confirmation and support.

For any queries, feel free to reach out to us via our contact page or directly through WhatsApp. We're here to make your event memorable with delicious food and smooth service!
